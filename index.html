Clear Lake Webmap

Open the Interactive Map

How to use

  • Click a colored parcel to see the claimant name, patent date, and patent type.
  • Use the layer list/legend to turn claim types on or off.
  • Patent links open in a new browser tab; close the patent tab to return to the map.

Scope and limitations

This map includes selected early federal land claims in the Clear Lake study area under three authorities: Homestead, Purchased Land (Cash Entry), and Military/Scrip Warrant. It does not represent all federal patent authorities or all land transfers. Boundaries are approximate and intended for historical visualization only.

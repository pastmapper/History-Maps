# Clearlake-Webmap
About This Map

This interactive map shows historic federal land claims around Clear Lake, Skagit County, Washington. It includes homestead, cash entry (purchased land), and military/scrip warrant patents recorded by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) General Land Office.

Each land claim can be clicked to view the claimantâ€™s name, patent date, patent type, and a direct link to the original BLM land patent record.

Boundaries are approximate and intended for historical and educational use only. This map represents individuals who received land under these three patent authorities within the study area.

Data sources:
BLM General Land Office Records; Public Land Survey System (PLSS) cadastral data; OpenStreetMap contributors.

Created by: Corrinne Sande, 2026.

How to use:

Zoom and pan to explore the map

Use the legend to turn claim types on and off

Click a parcel to view details and open the original patent record
